About the Artist

Mandar Karnik’s work exists at the intersection of emotion, color, and inner experience. His paintings explore identity, silence, intensity, and optimism through bold palettes and expressive forms. Each artwork invites the viewer to pause, reflect, and interpret— allowing personal meaning to emerge naturally.

Moving fluidly between figurative and abstract language, Mandar’s practice embraces contrast: strength and vulnerability, chaos and calm, depth and light. His work does not demand answers— it opens conversations.
