About the Artist

Mandar Karnik is a visionary artist who pushes boundaries and explores the extraordinary. Through vibrant colors, bold forms, and surreal compositions, Mandar captures the unseen emotions, dualities, and cultural depths that resonate universally. His works invite viewers to journey beyond the ordinary, evoking curiosity, introspection, and a sense of wonder. Each piece tells a story, revealing intricate details and thought-provoking perspectives.
