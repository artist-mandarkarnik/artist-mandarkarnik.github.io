About the Artist

Mandar Karnik’s work exists at the intersection of emotion, identity, and perception. His paintings explore inner landscapes—where color becomes language and form dissolves into feeling. Each piece invites prolonged engagement rather than immediate interpretation.

Artist CV

Emerging contemporary artist working primarily in acrylic and mixed media.
Themes include identity, duality, emotion, and abstraction.
Open to juried exhibitions, gallery representation, and collaborative projects.
