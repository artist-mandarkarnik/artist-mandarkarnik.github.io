About the Artist

Mandar Karnik is an autistic artist who explores emotions, identity, and the beauty of colors through contemporary and abstract art. Working with bold colors, expressive forms, and unique perspectives, Mandar seeks to communicate positivity, cultural reflections, and the complex thought processes of the human mind. Mandar also works with autistic children to help them express themselves through art, creating a space for joy and creativity.
