About the Artist

Mandar Karnik is a visionary artist whose work explores emotion, color, and thought in extraordinary ways. Each piece tells a story beyond the ordinary, inviting viewers to dive into a world of introspection, wonder, and visual poetry. Through bold forms and vibrant hues, Mandar captures unseen depths of human experience, making the invisible visible.
